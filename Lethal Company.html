« LETHAL COMPANY »

If there's one word to describe Lethal Company, it would be chaos. It's a co-op horror game where the main goal is to collect scrap from creepy buildings and sell it for profit. It might sound easy, but trust me— it's not. There's something terrifying waiting around the corner, and the jumpscare are absolutely brutal. It can be hilarious when you play with your friends and it happens to them— absolute top tier.
The graphics are decent, but the real fun comes from playing with your friends. Solo mode is very terrifying. Multiplayer is where the game shines— you will be screaming, running around, and laughing at 3am with your friends and goes completely out of your mind.
