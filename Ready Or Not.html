« READY OR NOT »

Ready or Not is not for the begginners. It's a tactical FPS where you play as part of a SWAT team, clearing missions, rescuing hostages, and facing dangerous situations. This game is realistic and hard, but that's exactly what makes it fun and rewarding.
The graphics are top-notch, and I love the fact that every mission feels different. Sometimes we succeed, sometimes we fail horribly— but it's exciting and always keeps us on the edge. I usually play this game with my buddies, especially at night, because the intense vibe just hits different when the lights are off.
