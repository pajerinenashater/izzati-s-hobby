« COOKING SIMULATOR »

Cooking Simulator is one of my favourite games I would play to relax though my kitchen looks completely chaos. In the game, you have to run a kitchen, prepare dishes, chop ingredients, fry and bake. Sometimes I accidently set things on fire and it's absolutely chaotic.
The graphics are great, and the cooking mechanics feel super realistic. But honestly, I found it quite boring after a while since it's a single-player game. Still, when I just want to chill or feel bored, this game is perfect.
