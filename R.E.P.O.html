« R.E.P.O »

R.E.P.O is one of my favourite games to play in middle of the night at 2a.m until the sun comes out. The goal of the game is to collect objects from haunted places and sell them— but there's a catch: the ghosts aren't exactly like us taking their stuff.
Each ghost behaves differently, so it's not just about running around, but also about knowing when to hide, when to risk it, and when to straight up run for your life.
The graphics are solid, and the game is really fun to play with friends— we panic, we scream for our lives and somehow still laugh through the fear.
